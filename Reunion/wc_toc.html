Introduction
These web cards present an HTML view of my genealogical database.

It contains a reasonably complete view of the Nierstrasz family tree, large fragments the Hahn tree (relatives of my paternal grandmother, Jet Hahn), and parts of the Margiotta family tree (relatives of my wife, Angela Margiotta). I would be grateful for additions and corrections. I am especially interested in any information about current generations of Nierstraszes, or in distant branches that have emigrated from the Netherlands (possibly using different spellings of the name).

If there is information here concerning you or your immediate family that you would prefer to keep private, please do not hesitate to contact me, and I will remove it.
